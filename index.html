Overview

Prevent is part of a wider government initiative called 'contest' to reduce the risk of terrorism in the UK. It is the safeguarding arm of the national counter terrorism strategy; the main aim of which is to stop people being drawn into or supporting extremism or terrorism

Prevent is a mutli-agency initiative that involves groups across all of society including: police, local authorities, educational institutions, and local community groups including the Channel Panel
